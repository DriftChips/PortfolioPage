ABOUT ME

Greetings! I'm Dmitry, a Software Engineer with foundational skills in graphic design and game development.


My professional journey commenced with a Graphic Design degree, yet I diverged from the conventional path. Opting for a more challenging route, I centered my projects around Virtual Reality and software development rather than traditional design.
